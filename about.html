    ABOUT

    The Coding Club was built to bring about coding skills within every individual. Inexperienced and experienced coders are welcome to join! With multiple events happening during the year, we guarantee a fun learning journey for all participants. Our Executive Members are experienced students and employees who have your best interest in mind. Members and students themselves are there to guide and assist you throughout your coding journey.

    MISSION

    Our mission is to empower students to enhance their coding skills, collaborate on projects, make valuable connections, and build strong portfolios. We aim to cultivate a community of innovative and passionate coders.
